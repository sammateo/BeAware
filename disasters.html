A hurricane is a type of storm called a tropical cyclone, which forms over tropical or subtropical waters.

A volcano is an opening in the earth's crust through which lava, volcanic ash, and gases escape.

An earthquake is an intense shaking of Earth's surface. The shaking is caused by movements in Earth's outermost layer.

A tsunami is a series of waves caused by earthquakes or undersea volcanic eruptions.
